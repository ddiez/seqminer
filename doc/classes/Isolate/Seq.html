splicing()

This method is very simple since we assume that there is not exon information in isolated sequences. This might be revisited in the future and then the method will mimic the one in Genome::Gene. But right now, it just takes into account the from and to values, plus the strandness. In fact, I am not sure if isolate sequences use different strands at all (which makes the whole thing even simpler).
